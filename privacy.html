Privacy Policy

Effective Date:11/01/2024

Lulu Discover (“we,” “us,” “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment information, when you sign up, rent devices, or contact us for support.
  • References: If required, we may collect contact information of references you provide to verify your credibility.
  • Usage Information: We may collect non-personal information about your interactions with our services, such as device usage data, website analytics, and cookies.
  • Images and Videos: We may store images and videos returned on rented devices or those provided to us for social media purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To process your rentals, verify identity, respond to inquiries, and provide customer support.
  • Content Sharing: To use images and videos you make available to us for social media or marketing, as permitted.
  • Communication: To send you updates, service information, or promotional materials related to Lulu Discover, if you have opted in.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage and improve our services, troubleshoot, and enhance the user experience.
  • Compliance: To comply with legal requirements and respond to lawful requests, subpoenas, or court orders.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party providers who assist us with payment processing, verification, analytics, and service delivery. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and may not use your information for other purposes.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information as required by law, to enforce our policies, or to protect rights, safety, and property.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred to the acquiring company.

4. Security of Your Information

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Personal information related to rentals and references is retained for verification and service continuity. Images and videos returned on rented devices or shared for social media may also be retained for marketing or archival purposes as permitted.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and Correction: You may access and correct your personal information by contacting us at hello@luludiscover.com.
  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of the information we hold about you in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent to data processing at any time. This may limit your ability to use some services.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and offer tailored content. You may adjust your browser settings to decline cookies, but this may impact your access to certain features of our services.

8. Children’s Privacy

Lulu Discover’s services are intended for individuals over the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information from our records.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We will ensure such transfers comply with applicable laws and that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

10. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website. Continued use of our services after changes are posted will constitute your acceptance of the revised policy.
